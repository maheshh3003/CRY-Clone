Today marks a historic milestone in our journey. We are thrilled to announce that CRY India has successfully reached over 2 million children across the country, transforming their lives through education, healthcare, and protection initiatives.

The Journey That Started 50 Years Ago

When CRY was founded in 1979, we had a simple yet powerful vision: to ensure that every child in India has access to their fundamental rights. What began as a small initiative has now grown into a movement that touches millions of lives.

Our Mission

To restore to children their basic rights: the right to childhood, the right to education, the right to healthcare, and the right to protection from exploitation.

Breaking Down the Numbers

Stories That Inspire

Behind every statistic is a story of transformation. Here are just a few examples of how your support has created lasting change:

Priya's Journey to Engineering

From a slum in Mumbai to becoming an engineer, Priya's story shows how education can transform lives. With CRY's support, she completed her schooling and is now pursuing her dream career.

Village Health Transformation

In rural Rajasthan, our healthcare initiatives reduced child mortality by 60% and improved nutrition levels significantly across 50 villages.

Looking Ahead: The Next Million

While we celebrate this incredible milestone, we know our work is far from over. Millions of children still need our help, and we're committed to reaching them all.

Our Goals for 2025-2030

  • Reach 3 million children by 2030
  • Expand digital education initiatives
  • Strengthen child protection systems
  • Enhance emergency response capabilities
  • Build sustainable community partnerships
