Why Volunteer With CRY?

Volunteering is such a simple, yet rewarding, way to support a cause you care about. While there are a million reasons why you should volunteer with CRY, here are our top 3 ones!

Make Real Impact

Your work, whether on the field or behind the scenes, will help us make India a better place for our children.

Develop Skills

Your volunteering experience will help you develop invaluable professional skills and build your leadership capabilities.

Flexible Options

No matter who you are or what you do, we have a wide range of volunteering options for you.

Our Volunteering Programs

Choose from our diverse range of volunteering opportunities that suit your schedule and skills

Onground & Online

Field Volunteering

Work directly with children and communities in the field. Engage in educational activities, health camps, and community development programs.

  • Direct interaction with children
  • Field visits and community work
  • Educational support activities
  • Health and nutrition programs
20+ hours/month 6+ months commitment
Onground Online Hybrid

Hybrid Volunteering

Combine online and offline activities. Perfect for those who want flexibility while making a meaningful impact through both digital and field work.

  • Content creation and social media
  • Virtual mentoring sessions
  • Event organization and support
  • Occasional field activities
10+ hours/month 3+ months commitment
